Irene Rojas is a developer with extensive experience in photography and video production. She will complete a George Washington University full-stack coding bootcamp in November 2018. She graduated with a bachelors in English from The College of William & Mary, and a masters in journalism from the University of Missouri. She has operated an independent media company for over eight years. She is eager to use her photography and video production background to create visually engaging websites. She is familiar with maintaining complicated technical equipment, as well as coordinating logistics with project stakeholders and third parties. She speaks English and Spanish. She is passionate about using technology to connect organizations with target audiences and users. She credits her freelance background with making her comfortable working independently and in a team environment. She looks forward to combining her coding and media skills, including JavaScripts, HTML, and CSS to create impactful and persuasive projects.
